French Onion Soup

Ingredients

  • 100g butter
  • 1.25kg yellow onions
  • 185ml (3/4 cup) dry white wine
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1.5 litres (6 cups) vegetable stock
  • 2 dried bay leaves
  • 3 sprigs fresh thyme
  • Salt and pepper

For the toast:

  • 6-8 slices french baguette
  • 100g gruyere/mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh parsley leaves

Directions

  1. Melt butter in a pan, when it starts to foam, add onions and stir.
  2. Cook for 30 min on medium low heat stirring every few minutes, until onions are soft and semi transparent.
  3. Turn heat up tomedium or medium high. Add salt and cook for a further 20-30 minutes, stirring regularly until onions are deep golden.
  4. Add wine, then simmer rapidly for 2 minutes until mostly evaporated, stirring to scrape the bottom of the pot.
  5. Sprinkle flour over the onion and cook for 1 minute.
  6. Add stock, thyme and bay leaves.
  7. Cover, lower heat so it's simmering gently then simmer for 30 minutes.
  8. Serve: Season to taste with salt and pepper. Ladle into bowls. Top with 1 or 2 slices of cheesy toast.

    Cheese toast:

  1. Preheat oven to high. Toast each side of bread until light golden.
  2. Top bread with cheese, then grill until melted and some brown spots appear.
